🎬 About This Project

A love letter to two worlds of storytelling.

What is "Toy Story OVA: Anime Showdown"?

Toy Story OVA: Anime Showdown is an original fan fiction story that imagines what would happen if six legendary anime action figures — Goku, Naruto, Luffy, Ichigo, Deku, and Tanjiro — woke up in Bonnie's room and met Woody, Buzz Lightyear, and the rest of the gang.

It's a story about identity, purpose, and what it means to be a hero — whether you're a toy, a warrior, or something in between. Written as a six-chapter "OVA" (Original Video Animation) in the spirit of anime specials, it blends Pixar's heartfelt storytelling with shonen anime's electric energy.

This is a non-commercial fan project created out of love for both Pixar and anime. All characters belong to their respective creators.
